About Me

Hello! I'm Aries, I am a 19 year old, Illinois based Illustrator, Sound designer and Video editor. I love filming editing and working with sound. One thing to know about me is that when given creative freedom I am a very comedic person, given the chance I love to impliment comedy in my work. When the world shut down during the pandemic, I really started dabbling into the arts and fell in love with entertaining. One of the things I love about music, animation, and entertainment as a whole, is that it allows you to escape reality and the stress everyday life can bring. A career goal of mine is to help create that escape for others. With everyday life, comes stress, but everyone deserves to take a break every once in a while and escape to a world that brings them joy.
